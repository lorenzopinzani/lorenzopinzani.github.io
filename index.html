Lorenzo Pinzani, PhD

Postdoctoral Researcher in Plant Ecology & Biodiversity
Roma Tre University, Rome (Italy)

Plant ecologist and botanist specialising in Mediterranean vascular flora, with a focus on freshwater and wetland ecosystems. My research combines field-based floristic surveys, ecological analysis, and georeferenced data to support biodiversity monitoring, conservation, and the study of plant distribution patterns and biological invasions.
